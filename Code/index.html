What is MyGuardian?

An easy, cost-effective standalone vehicle entry and exit system that simplifies life for everyone in a gated community, from residents and management committee members to security guards and facility managers. It lets you control which vehicles can access your parking lot.

How It Works?

The system automatically handles all vehicle entry decision steps, i.e.: vehicle image capture, vehicle list management, access decisions and barrier control.

It is a small, camera-based system, it automatically manages access by verifying license plates. When a vehicle enters a detection zone, the camera captures an image of its license plate through automatic number plate recognition technique. The analytics application verifies the images against lists of authorized and unauthorized license plates stored in the camera.

The camera sends its decision to the barrier via its internal I/O or external I/O. The barrier executes the open/close decision Entry decisions are based solely on license plate verification. Event log options include up to 1000 entries on camera storage and include thumbnail images of license plates for easy follow-up investigations.
